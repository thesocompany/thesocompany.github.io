Shaun So and Richard Wheeler met in a fox hole. Shaun and Richard both joined the military after 9/11 and first crossed paths in Army basic training. A friendship, paired with their complementary yet diverse backgrounds, formed the foundation of The So Company—a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business focused on making government better.

  • Shaun So

    Shaun So

    Prior to building The So Company, which is his second company, Shaun spent over a decade working for a variety of U.S. federal government agencies. He was an intelligence analyst for the Joint Staff in the Pentagon; a counterintelligence special agent for a variety of stateside and overseas special-mission units; a non-commissioned officer in the U.S. Army Reserves; and a U.S. intelligence operations support officer for the Department of Defense.

    In 2011, after selling his first company to a NASDAQ-traded company, he decided to combine his public-sector experience with his recent experiences in innovation to solve hard civic problems. The So Company exists to privide services to organizations at the intersection of private-sector best practices and government change management.

    Shaun graduated with honors from the University of Chicago and has an MBA from CUNY Baruch, Zicklin School of Business. He's written on a variety of topics involving entrepreneurship, Veterans, and innovation at Forbes.
